# PRESS RELEASE

## Israeli AI Startup ShiputzAI Disrupts Traditional Renovation Planning with Computer Vision Technology

**Tel Aviv, Israel — April 2, 2026** — ShiputzAI (shipazti.com) today announced new AI capabilities that are reshaping how Israelis plan and budget home renovations. The platform now features AI-powered quote analysis that helps homeowners detect overcharging in contractor bids, alongside automated bill of quantities generation.

### Solving Renovation's Biggest Pain Point: Cost Uncertainty

According to Israeli consumer surveys, 67% of homeowners experience cost overruns during renovation projects, with the average overspend reaching 35% above initial estimates. ShiputzAI addresses this with two AI-powered tools:

- **AI Quote Analysis**: Upload a contractor's quote and receive an instant assessment of whether prices are reasonable compared to market rates. The AI identifies line items that appear overpriced and suggests questions to ask your contractor.

- **Automated Bill of Quantities (כתב כמויות)**: Upload a room photo and receive a detailed, itemized cost breakdown for the renovation — materials, labor, and timeline — generated in seconds rather than the days typically required for manual estimation.

### Seven Tools, One Platform

ShiputzAI's full toolkit includes AI room visualization, style matching (AI identifies your preferred design style), Shop the Look (click to buy furniture from any room photo), AI video tours, smart floorplans, bill of quantities, and quote analysis.

"We're not just another room visualization tool," said Guy Cezana, founder. "ShiputzAI covers the entire renovation journey — from inspiration to budgeting to contractor management. Everything in Hebrew, designed for Israeli construction standards and pricing."

### Traction and Growth

Since launch, ShiputzAI has processed thousands of AI visualizations and serves a growing base of homeowners, interior designers, and contractors across Israel. The platform is available at [shipazti.com](https://shipazti.com) with pricing starting at ₪29.
