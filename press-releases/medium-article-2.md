## Where AI Wins

### 1. Speed
A traditional designer needs 2-4 weeks to produce a room visualization. ShiputzAI generates one in 15-30 seconds. You can try 10 different styles in the time it takes to drink a coffee.

### 2. Cost
A single room visualization from a designer costs ₪2,000-₪5,000. With ShiputzAI, it's ₪3-₪15. That's a 100-300x cost reduction.

### 3. Experimentation
With a designer, you get 2-3 options and each revision costs money. With AI, you can generate 50 different versions of your kitchen for the price of one designer consultation.

### 4. Bill of Quantities
Most designers charge separately for a bill of quantities (כתב כמויות). ShiputzAI generates one automatically from your room photo — materials, labor, timeline, costs.

### 5. Quote Analysis
No designer helps you analyze contractor quotes. ShiputzAI's AI reads your quote and flags overpriced items compared to market rates.

## Where Traditional Designers Win

### 1. Custom Furniture & Millwork
AI can't design custom built-in closets or a unique dining table for your specific space. Designers can.

### 2. 3D Technical Plans
For construction-grade drawings, you need an architect or designer with CAD software. AI visualizations are for inspiration, not construction.

### 3. Material Selection
Designers physically visit showrooms, touch fabrics, and match colors in person. AI works with digital approximations.

### 4. Project Management
A designer manages the contractor, timeline, and budget throughout the renovation. AI gives you tools, but you still manage the process.

## The Smart Approach: Use Both

The most cost-effective approach in 2026 is to combine both:

1. **Start with AI** — Use ShiputzAI to explore styles, generate visualizations, and create a bill of quantities (₪30-₪150)
2. **Refine with a designer** — Bring your AI visualizations to a designer for custom refinements (saves 50-70% of their time)
3. **Manage with AI** — Use quote analysis and cost estimates to keep the project on budget

This hybrid approach can save 40-60% compared to using a designer for everything, while still getting professional-quality results.
