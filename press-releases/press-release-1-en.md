# PRESS RELEASE

## ShiputzAI Launches Israel's First AI-Powered Renovation Visualization Platform with 7 Integrated Tools

**Tel Aviv, Israel — April 2, 2026** — ShiputzAI (shipazti.com), an Israeli startup, has launched the country's first comprehensive AI-powered renovation and interior design visualization platform. Unlike existing tools that offer basic room rendering, ShiputzAI integrates seven specialized AI tools in a single Hebrew-language platform designed specifically for the Israeli market.

### What Makes ShiputzAI Different

ShiputzAI is the only platform offering all of the following in one interface:

1. **AI Room Visualization** — Upload a photo and see your room redesigned in seconds
2. **Style Matcher** — AI identifies your design style and creates a shopping list
3. **Shop the Look** — Click on any item in a room photo to find where to buy it
4. **AI Video Tour** — Generate a walkthrough video of your redesigned space
5. **Smart Floorplan** — AI-generated architectural floor plans from room photos
6. **Bill of Quantities** — Automated cost estimation for renovation projects
7. **Quote Analysis** — AI-powered analysis of contractor quotes to prevent overcharging

"Most AI interior design tools do one thing — basic room rendering," said Guy Cezana, founder of ShiputzAI. "We built a complete renovation toolkit. An Israeli homeowner can visualize their kitchen renovation, generate a bill of quantities, get a cost estimate, and analyze contractor quotes — all in Hebrew, all in one platform."

### Addressing a $12 Billion Market

Israel's renovation market exceeds ₪40 billion annually, with over 200,000 renovation projects started each year. ShiputzAI targets homeowners, interior designers, architects, and contractors who need fast, affordable visualization before committing to expensive renovations.

The platform uses Google's Gemini AI for image generation and analysis, producing photorealistic renovation visualizations in under 15 seconds. Pricing starts at ₪29 for 10 AI credits, with a free tier of 10 credits upon signup.

### Availability

ShiputzAI is available now at [shipazti.com](https://shipazti.com). The platform supports Hebrew and English, with a mobile-optimized interface.
